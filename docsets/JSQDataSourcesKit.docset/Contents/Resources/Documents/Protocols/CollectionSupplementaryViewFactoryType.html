CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType

An instance conforming to CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType is responsible for initializing and configuring collection view supplementary views to be consumed by an instance of CollectionViewDataSourceProvider. The CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType protocol has two associated types, DataItem and SupplementaryView. These associated types describe the type of model instances backing the collection view and the type of supplementary views in the collection view, respectively.
