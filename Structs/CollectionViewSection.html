CollectionViewSection

public struct CollectionViewSection <Item>: CollectionViewSectionInfo

A CollectionViewSection is a concrete CollectionViewSectionInfo. A section instance is responsible for managing the elements in a section. Elements in the section may be accessed or replaced via its subscripting interface.
